About NER

"What is NER?". Named Entity Recognition (NER) is a form of Natural Language Processing (NLP) that involves extracting and identifying essential information from text. The information that is extracted and categorised is called an entity. It can be any word or series of word that is used as a Named Entity, i.e. Person, Organisation and Location.

(SABDA NER is a Nepali language based NER application.)

Our Model

SABDA-NER

Features of SABDA NER
  • Detecting the entities from the text
  • Classifying entities into different categories, i.e. Person, Organisation and Location
  • Converts unstructured information into structured data
  • Bestows structure upon raw textual data extracting valuable insights
  • Enables downstream analyses
Potential Applications
  • Location Tracking
  • Organisational Evalutaion
  • News and Media Analysis
  • General public & Public figure mentions
  • Data Contextualisation
